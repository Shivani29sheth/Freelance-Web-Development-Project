Corporate Social Responsibility(CSR)

At Raj Farms, Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) is embedded in its long term business strategy. For Raj Farms, business priorities co-exist with social commitments to drive holistic development of people and the environment. As most of us know, Raj Farms, is a privately owned property and has been recently opened for renting out purposes, be it, hosting parties, celebrations etc. Major chunk of the proceeds from these goes to the NGO, “Gramin Avam Parvatiya Utthan Samiti (Reg.)” promoted by the owner Shri Rajesh Pant. The NGO works towards the upliftment of rural women in the field of education, healthcare and in their day-to-day activities. It is focussed on improving their quality of life, thus, enabling them to become independent citizens with better future.
Furthermore, Raj Farms Group is actively committed towards preserving and maintaining a greener and healthier environment. With the objective of making a positive and long-term sustainable impact on the community, we at Raj Farms strive towards "minimizing the use of non renewable sources of energy, through installing solar panels," thus, reducing and minimizing the use of non renewable sources of energy. The whole farm is run through solar energy tapped through solar panels. Thus, we, in our small capacity aim at a greener and cleaner world for generations to come.
